## 🏆 What is it?

In 38-0, you draft and build your own Premier League squad with one goal: assemble a team strong enough to emulate the unbeaten season. Pick players, balance your XI, and see if your squad could go the distance.

It taps into one of football's most romantic ideas — the perfect, unbeatable season. Arsenal achieved it in 2003-04; now you can try to build a squad capable of the same.
